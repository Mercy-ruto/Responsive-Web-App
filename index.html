About Us

We are a group of young, innovative students studying in various universities across Australia. Our common hobbies and leisure activities bring us together and we continue exploring life in our academics and fun filled activities during our free time and monthly meetups. Want to know more read on.

Club Formation

This club was formed in the year 2020 by our two patron members on their vacation in Canada then being great friends. Thereafter, following a successful vacation, they decided to come up with efforts to bring in like minded students, pursuing computer science and other Information technolgy fields. In the year 2021, 10 new members had already joined increasing the strength of the young association which in the following years to date has seen its evolution into a giant student Tech Club among Australian students.
